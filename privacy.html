Change Order

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 23, 2026

Change Order is a tool for contractors to create, send, and collect client approval on extra work. Because the app involves accounts, uploaded photos, and client signatures, we want to be clear about what data we collect and how we handle it.

Information you provide

When you create an account we collect your email address. When you create change orders we store the information you enter: project names, client names and email addresses, scope descriptions, dollar amounts, and any photos you attach. When a client approves or rejects a change order through the secure review link, we store their typed signature name and optional note.

We do not collect your payment card details. Payments are processed directly by Apple, Google, or Stripe, as described below.

How we use your information

  • To operate the app — storing your change orders, photos, and approval records so you can access them across devices.
  • To deliver the client review experience — sending your client a secure link and recording their response.
  • To send transactional emails — for example, notifying a client that a change order is awaiting their review (only if you provide their email and email delivery is configured).
  • To send push notifications to your device — for example, when a client approves or rejects a change order.
  • To improve the app — through anonymous, aggregate analytics described below.

Data storage (Supabase)

Your account, change order data, and uploaded photos are stored on Supabase (hosted on Amazon Web Services, US-East region). Supabase uses encryption at rest and in transit. See Supabase's Privacy Policy for details.

Analytics (PostHog)

We use PostHog to collect anonymous, aggregate usage data — for example, which screens are visited and how quickly actions complete. No change order content, client names, dollar amounts, or any business data are ever sent to PostHog. On web, analytics are only collected after you grant cookie consent. On mobile, app installation constitutes consent, consistent with Apple and Google platform policies.

See PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Crash reporting (Sentry)

If the app crashes, an anonymous crash report is sent to Sentry. Reports include the device OS version, app version, and a stack trace. Change order content and personally identifiable information are excluded from crash reports. See Sentry's Privacy Policy.

Email delivery (Resend)

When you send a change order to a client by email, your client's email address and a link to the review page are passed to Resend for delivery. Resend does not receive change order contents. See Resend's Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions (Stripe and RevenueCat)

Plus subscriptions on web are processed by Stripe. Subscriptions on iOS and Android are processed by Apple or Google through in-app purchase, managed by RevenueCat. None of these services receive your change order data. Your payment card details are handled entirely by the payment processor and are never stored by Change Order.

See Stripe's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your change order data, client information, or photos with any third party except as necessary to operate the service (the processors listed above) or as required by law.

When you share a change order review link with your client, that link grants access to the specific change order details you created. That access is limited to the order contents and does not include your account or other orders.

Data retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from Settings → Privacy & account → Delete account. Deletion removes your profile, change orders, and photos from our systems. Some records may be retained where required by law (for example, for fraud prevention or legal compliance).

Children

Change Order is intended for use by contractors and business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the EEA, UK, and California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below or use the in-app account deletion feature. For data held by third-party processors, please contact those services directly.
